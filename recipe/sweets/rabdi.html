Rabadi

Prep Time

15 minutes

Cook Time

20 minutes

Total Time

35 minutes

Servings

4 people

Ingredients

    Hyderabadi Veg Biryani Ingredients Ingredients for Rabdi
    • Blanch Nuts (Optional)
      • Almonds: A small handful (approximately 10-12)
      • Pistachios: A small handful (approximately 10-12)
    • Cook to Reduce Milk
      • Full-Fat Whole Milk: 1.25 litres
    • Make Rabdi Recipe
      • Sugar: 2.5 to 3 tablespoons (adjust to taste)
      • Saffron Strands: 15 to 18 strands, crushed
      • Green Cardamom Powder: ½ teaspoon
      • Rose Water or Kewra Water (Pandanus Water): 1 teaspoon (optional)
    • Garnish
      • Blanched Almonds: Reserved from blanching
      • Blanched Pistachios: Reserved from blanching

Steps to Make Recipe

Before you begin heating and simmering milk, I suggest blanching the nuts and keeping them ready. This blanching step is optional, and you can simply sliver or chop the nuts. Blanching improves their texture and taste. Follow these steps:

  1. Soak the almonds and pistachios in hot water for 20 to 30 minutes. Then remove the peels.
  2. Slice, sliver, or chop the nuts and set aside.
  1. Add 1.25 litres of full-fat whole milk in a kadai, and slowly heat the milk on low to medium heat. Stir a few times as the milk heats up. The milk should slightly simmer but not boil. Once hot, the milk will start to froth or form a layer of cream (malai) on top.
  2. When you see the cream floating on top, use a spatula to gently move the cream layer to the side of the pot.
  3. Stick the cream to the side of the pan.
  1. Continue simmering and stirring the milk on low heat, collecting the cream layer on the sides and sticking it to the pan. This will take about an hour while the milk reduces, so keep a close eye to prevent burning or sticking to the bottom of the pan.
  2. Stir the milk a few times after moving the cream layer to the sides to balance between letting the cream form and preventing burning.
  3. After about 30 minutes, the milk should have reduced to half, and the layers of cream on the sides will have thickened.
  1. Once the milk has reduced to half, add 2.5 to 3 tablespoons of sugar to taste.
  2. Add 15 to 18 crushed saffron strands and ½ teaspoon of cardamom powder. Stir gently.
  3. Continue stirring gently, and collect the cream on the side of the pan. The rabdi will turn a lovely yellow shade once the saffron is fully incorporated.
  4. When the milk has reduced to ⅓ of its original quantity (approximately 1 hour 15 minutes), turn off the heat. You can also reduce the milk to ¼ of its original volume if desired.
  5. Scrape off the entire cream layer and any dried milk solids with a spatula, and add them back to the thickened milk.
  6. Stir gently.
  7. Add the sliced almonds and pistachios, and 1 teaspoon of rose water or kewra water (optional). Stir and mix gently. Reserve a few blanched nuts for garnish.

Recipe Tips

Stirring: Regular stirring helps avoid burning and ensures even reduction. Be patient; this process takes about 1 to 1.5 hours. Consistency: Reduce milk to your preferred thickness. Rabdi thickens more as it cools. Final Touch: Scrape and add cream layers back to the milk for rich texture and flavor.
